Pacific Rhododendron

Plant Species Name:

Pacific Rhodendron

Scientific Name:

Rhododendron macrophyllum

Common Alternate Names:

  • Coast Rhododendron
  • California Rosebay

Range of Physical Size of the Plant Species:

3 - 15 feet in height.

Where the Plant Can be Found:

They can be found in Canada all the way to California, USA. They grow "west of the Cascades crest and at the coast in Washington."

Best Climate to Grow these Plants:

Locations with cool winters and mild summers are ideal to grow these plants. The minimum temperature required has a range between -10 °F - 30 °F.
