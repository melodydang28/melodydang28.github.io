SMOSH

About

Smosh was created by two best friends, Ian and Anthony, in 2005.They first started creating lipsyncing videos to popular children TV shows and movies like Power Rangers, Mortal Kombat, and Pok√©mon on Smosh.com, but they later moved to posting on YouTube instead. One of Smosh's earliest YouTube posts, Pokemon Theme Song in November 28, 2005, became the most viewed YouTube video with 4,300,000 views at the time.

As Smosh grew, more regular cast members were introduced in 2015. Keith Leak II, Noah Grossman, Olivia Sui, Courtney Miller, and Shayne Topp were incorporated into most of the videos and are still prominent cast members in the current cast. 2 years later Anothony left Smosh to pursue more creative outlooks on YouTube. Along with a few changes, they introduced their first game show, "You Posted That?" and featured other YouTubers as contestants.

Between 2019 and 2023, Smosh was owned by Rhett and Link through Mythical Entertainment. They also introduced a sub channel, Smosh Pit, along with more new regular cast members, Damien Haas, Spencer Agnew, Tommy Bowe, Amanda-Lehan-Canto, Kimmy Jimenez(left in 2023), Jacklyn Uweh(left in 2023), Chanse McCrary, Angela Giarratana, and Arasha Lalani. Through this ownership Smosh continued to post seperate content that wasn't directly intertwined with Rhett and Link. In the second half of 2023 Ian and Anthony bought most of Smosh from Mythical and started producing and directing more comedy sketches, more game related videos, and new shows.

Achievments

  • Youtube Lifetime Achievment Award - November, 2025

    This award recognizes their 20 years of content creation and their role as trailblazers of YouTube.

  • Shorty Impact Award - October, 2025

  • 1,000,000 Subscribers - August, 2009

    Smosh was one of the first YouTube channels to reach 1 million subscribers on the platform.

Guinness World Records

  • Most viewed fan film based on the Legend of Zelda video-game franchise. In March, 2017 they achieved this with 68 million views.
  • Most viewed fan-film based on an action/adventure video game. In March, 2015 they achieved this with their Ultimate Assassin's Creed Song Music Video with 61 million views.
  • The 1st YouTube channel to reach 1,000 subscribers. Acheived in November, 2005.
  • The 1st YouTube channel to reach 100,000 subscribers. Acheived in 2007.
  • The 1st YouTube channel to reach 10,000,000 subscribers. Acheived in May, 2013.
  • Fastest Time to Clear a Game of Hungry Hungry Hippo in teams of 2. On August 21st, 2025, Shayne and Chanse beat this record with 8.03 seconds.
