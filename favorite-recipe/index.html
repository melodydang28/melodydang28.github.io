Crab Rangoons

Easy to make, scrumptious crab rangoons. Your perfect appetizer!

Ingredients, for about 20 wontons

  • 1 8-ounce package cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of cooked crab meat
  • 1/2 teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 package of wonton wrappers
  • 1 Small bowl with a layer of water, for folding
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Sweet and Sour Sauce, for dipping

Directions

  1. Add your cream cheese, shredded crab, salt, and black pepper into a medium bowl and mix them together.
  2. Use parchment paper and line the bottom of a baking sheet.
  3. Place 1 tablespoon of filling in the middle of a wonton wrapper. Dip your finger in the small bowl of water and trace the sides of the inside of the wonton.
  4. Fold the sides of the wrapper in half so the corners are touching. Refer to the image for visual guidance.
  5. In a wok, add oil to about 3 inches deep. Heat the oil over medium heat to 350 degrees F. Fry the wontons in batches, making sure it doesn't over crowd, for about 2-3 minutes, until golden brown.
  6. Transfer them to a plate lined with paper towels and continue frying the rest of the wontons in batches.
  7. Let them cool down, then serve them with the Sweet and Sour Sauce.
