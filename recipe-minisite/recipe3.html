Brownies

Total Time: 45 mins

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 30 mins

Yield: 16 pieces

Ingredients, for 16 pieces

  • 10 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 ¼ cups of granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup and 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon of sea salt
  • 2 large cold eggs
  • ½ cup of all-purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup of chopped walnuts, pecans, or chocolate chips (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Line the bottom and sides of an 8-inch square baking pan with aluminum foil or parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat until the butter melts completely, making sure not to let it brown.
  3. While the butter is still hot, stir in the sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, and salt. Stir well until blended.
  4. Set the saucepan aside to cool until the mixture is warm (5-10 minutes).
  5. Add the cold eggs, one at a time, stirring vigorously throughout.
  6. When the batter looks thick and well blended, add in the flour. Use a spatula to beat the flour into the batter until it becomes very thick and pulls away from the sides of the bowl.
  7. Add in your optional mix-ins.
  8. Pour in your batter into the prepped pan and spread it evenly, pushing into the corners. Then bake it for 20-30 minutes for moist brownies.
